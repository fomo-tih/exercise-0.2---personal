Television Energy Consumption in Australia

Modern LED and OLED televisions are much more efficient than older plasma models. On average:

  • Small LED TV (32”): ~60 kWh per year
  • Mid-size LED TV (50”): ~120 kWh per year
  • Large Plasma TV (60”+): 300–400 kWh per year

Choosing energy-efficient models can save households up to $100 per year in electricity costs and reduce overall demand on the Australian energy grid.
